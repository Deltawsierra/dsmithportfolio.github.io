Beyond the Resume

Hello there! Welcome to a little about me that's beyond just the resume.
I'm a dynamic fusion of tech-savvy and geek culture enthusiast, who finds joy in the realms of coding and fantasy alike. When I'm not working or programming some game passion project, I'm probably rolling dice and leading adventures in Dungeons & Dragons, immersing myself in the world of my creation (Endrazia) in one of the two campaigns I DM for, one that's been going on for two years and the other for nine years.
When it’s not tabletop then I'm probably playing one of my favorite video games like Fable 2 or Baldur's Gate 3, or gaming with friends in League of Legends, Civilization 6, or Super Smash Bros Melee. My love for gaming is matched by my passion for sports; some of my favorites being soccer, lacrosse, basketball, or ultimate frisbee. I'm all about the thrill of the game.
Music fuels me, tunes from Against The Current and Bring Me The Horizon setting the backdrop for many coding sessions, while Fall Out Boy and Green Day remind me of my roots, admittedly my taste is mostly stuck in 2000’s alt rock and metal. An exception to that though is Taylor Swift and her lyrical storytelling, which somehow, finds a way to resonate with my own narrative.
My movie preferences? The epic tales of Lord of the Rings and Troy, the heroic stories of The Amazing Spider-Man (Andrew Garfield has my vote), the magical world of Harry Potter (Goblet of Fire is my top pick), to the quirky and hauntingly beautiful The Nightmare Before Christmas.
Other random stuff? Food: tacos, lobster mac and cheese, and spaghetti and meatballs are my top comfort foods. When I’m not in front of a computer or Tv? I'm likely at the gym working out, or hosting board game nights with friends which can get VERY competitive. At college I’m an active member of the gaming club and the volleyball club.
This is a small blend telling who I am: a tech enthusiast shaped by gaming, sports, music, and movies. I bring this diverse perspective to everything I do, driven by a passion for creating, exploring, and making every adventure, whether in code or in life, truly epic.
